MURDER MYSTERY GAME — MASTER EVIDENCE CARD LIST
=================================================
Purpose: Master reference list for the web app engine to assemble unique crime scenarios.
Each card is generic enough to be reused across many different crime types (murder,
poisoning, arson, kidnapping, staged suicide, etc.). Cards can serve as genuine clues
OR red herrings. Players may submit cards for forensic testing (DNA, fingerprints,
digital forensics, chemical analysis, etc.) via the web app.
